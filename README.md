## Research Interests

I am a computational biologist with experience in single-cell transcriptomics and cancer biology. My research focuses on quantifying transcriptional plasticity in tumour cell populations — how individual cancer cells switch between gene expression states, and what that means for metastasis and immune evasion.

My broader interests sit at the intersection of:
- Single-cell RNA sequencing analysis and method development
- Cancer transcriptomics and tumour heterogeneity
- Tumour microenvironment composition and immune-tumour crosstalk
- Information-theoretic approaches to quantifying biological complexity
